2-Year Residential Program

Center of Excellence (CoE)

Intensive classroom coaching for JEE & NEET inside Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas

Ex Navodayan Foundation offers a comprehensive 2-year coaching program exclusively to students of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs). Students enroll by qualifying the Navodaya Dakshana Selection Test (NDST) organized by Dakshana Foundation. Selected students are relocated to designated JNV centres where ENF conducts its CoE classes with a dedicated team of expert faculty in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology, alongside program coordinators who reside at the centres full-time. ENF also assists students with counseling for their desired institutes and provides scholarships to deserving students.

Online Learning at Scale

ENABLE

Engineering NEET Admission Bridge Accelerated Learning Engagement — reaching 325+ EMRS across India

ENABLE is a joint initiative of Tata Motors, National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS), and Ex Navodayan Foundation, launched in January 2021. As the knowledge partner, ENF conducts regular online classes for JEE & NEET aspirants in Class 11 and Class 12 across 325+ Eklavya Model Residential Schools. ENABLE serves as the umbrella program covering five regional zones — providing equitable access to quality competitive exam preparation for students who would otherwise have limited resources.
