Our Vision, Their Legacy

Built on Purpose, Driven by Passion

Ex Navodayan Foundation was born from the shared vision of JNV alumni who believed that every Navodayan student deserves access to quality education and opportunities for excellence. Our founders and trustees bring together decades of experience in education, technology, finance, and community development to create lasting impact.

NS
Managing Trustee

Er. Nagendra Singh

Managing Trustee & Founder
B.Tech in Engineering
Ex-Navodayan, JNV Alumni
Education Management Social Work Strategic Leadership

Visionary leader who conceptualized and founded ENF in 2016 with a mission to empower JNV students. Under his leadership, ENF has grown from a small initiative to a nationwide movement impacting 15,000+ students across 380+ schools. Pioneered the CoE model and established strategic partnerships with Tata Motors, NVS, and DBSE.

Every Navodayan deserves the opportunity to dream big and achieve excellence.

Our Foundation

Our Founding Principles

The core beliefs that guide every decision, program, and initiative at Ex Navodayan Foundation.

Education for All

Every Navodayan student, regardless of socio-economic background, deserves access to quality education and career guidance to unlock their full potential.

Community First

The Navodayan alumni network is our greatest strength. We believe in giving back to the community that shaped us and nurturing the next generation.

Innovation & Excellence

Embracing technology and modern pedagogies to deliver world-class educational programs that prepare students for competitive examinations and careers.

Transparency & Trust

Operating with complete financial transparency, ethical governance, and accountability to our stakeholders, partners, and the students we serve.
