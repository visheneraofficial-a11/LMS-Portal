Our Partners

Organizations Driving Change Together

We proudly collaborate with government bodies, corporate houses, and social organizations committed to transforming education for underprivileged students across India.

Tata Motors

Since 2021 CSR Partner

Strategic CSR partner for the ENABLE program. A joint initiative to provide quality JEE/NEET preparation to Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya students across India.

  • Funding, technology, and resource support for 350+ JNVs
  • Covering 5 regions across the country
  • Quality JEE & NEET preparation for JNV students

Tata AIG General Insurance

Since 2019 CSR

Supporting ENF's educational initiatives with corporate social responsibility funding. A valued partner contributing to student welfare programs and scholarship support.

  • Corporate social responsibility funding since 2019
  • Student welfare programs and support
  • Scholarship contributions for deserving students

Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS)

Institutional Government

Government body under the Ministry of Education managing all 661 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas across India. Key institutional partner providing access to school infrastructure and student enrollment.

  • Managing 661 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas
  • Access to school infrastructure nationwide
  • Student enrollment and institutional support

Ministry of Science & Technology

Government STEM

Government partnership supporting STEM education and innovation initiatives. Programs for strengthening science education at the grassroots level across India.

  • STEM education and innovation programs
  • Strengthening science education at grassroots level
  • Research and innovation mentorship support

Social Welfare Society, UP

State Level Social Welfare

State-level partnership for social welfare and education outreach programs in Uttar Pradesh. Community engagement and support for underprivileged students.

  • Social welfare & education outreach in UP
  • Community engagement and student support
  • Programs for underprivileged communities

Tata Power Delhi Distribution Ltd (TPDDL)

Since Nov 2022 Knowledge

Knowledge partnership since November 2022. A joint education initiative providing quality education to underprivileged students in the Delhi NCR region through CSR initiatives.

  • Quality education for 150+ underprivileged students
  • Delhi NCR region CSR initiative
  • Joint education and knowledge partnership

Delhi Board of School Education (DBSE)

Since 2021 Education

Partnership with Delhi Government for 31 ASoSE (Atal Sreshtha Vidhyalaya) schools. JEE/NEET preparation programs empowering government school students.

  • 31 ASoSE (Atal Sreshtha Vidhyalaya) schools
  • JEE & NEET preparation programs
  • Empowering Delhi government school students
