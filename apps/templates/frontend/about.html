Who We Are

Transforming Education for Underprivileged Students

Ex Navodayan Foundation (ENF) is a premier not-for-profit Trust established in 2016 by JNV Alumni (Ex-Navodayans) and registered under the Indian Trust Act, based in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.

ENF is dedicated to supporting senior secondary students from underprivileged and marginalized backgrounds, helping them qualify for premier institutes of engineering and medicine through competitive examinations like JEE and NEET.

Since its inception, ENF has grown from supporting 50 students to reaching over 15,000 students across 380+ partner schools in 28+ states through flagship programs like CoE, ENABLE, TPDDL, ASOSE, and SEED.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Driving meaningful change in education through a clear purpose and an unwavering commitment to student empowerment.

Our Mission

Empowering underprivileged and talented students from Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) and government schools to achieve academic excellence, particularly in competitive examinations like JEE and NEET, through quality education, mentorship, and technology-enabled learning.

Our Vision

To build a comprehensive ecosystem where every deserving student from JNV and government schools can qualify JEE for IIT and NEET for AIIMS — irrespective of their socio-economic background.

Our Values

Our Journey

Key Milestones

A decade of growth, impact, and unwavering commitment to transforming education for underprivileged students across India.

2016

Foundation Established

ENF established by JNV Alumni in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, as a registered Trust under the Indian Trust Act.

2017

First Center of Excellence

Launched the first CoE (Center of Excellence) at JNV Meja, Prayagraj, providing intensive JEE/NEET coaching.

2019

Tata AIG Partnership

Secured partnership with Tata AIG General Insurance for CSR-funded educational initiatives and student welfare programs.

2020

Online Classes Launched

Launched online classes during COVID-19 pandemic, establishing the ENABLE PROGRAM platform for remote learning.

2021

ENABLE & DBSE Programs

ENABLE program launched with Tata Motors & NVS covering 350+ JNVs. DBSE partnership established with Delhi Government.

2022

TPDDL & ASOSE Programs

TPDDL program launched in November. ASOSE program started in August at Dr. BR Ambedkar School of Specialised Excellence.

2023

CoE Expansion

Expanded Center of Excellence to JNV Gajokhar, Varanasi, increasing capacity for intensive coaching programs.

2024

SEED Program Launched

SEED program launched at Tata Motors plant locations, expanding educational reach to new communities.

2025

Third CoE & AI Platform

Third CoE established at JNV Rajgir, Nalanda. AI-Powered learning platform launched for personalized education.
